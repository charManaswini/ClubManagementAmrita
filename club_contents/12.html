Geeks for Geeks club at Amrita University is a student-run organization that is dedicated to helping students learn and improve their technical skills. The club provides a platform for students to share their knowledge and expertise in programming languages, software engineering, and computer science. Through various activities such as seminars, workshops, hackathons, and coding challenges, members of the club are able to acquire new knowledge and hone their skills. The club also organizes events, such as guest lectures from industry experts, to help students gain valuable insight into the world of technology. Geeks for Geeks club provides students with a great opportunity to network and collaborate with like-minded people who are passionate about technology.
