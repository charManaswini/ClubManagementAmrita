Face Club at Amrita University is a student-run club that aims to create an inclusive, vibrant, and collaborative environment for students to explore their passions, interests, and skills in the field of face recognition technologies. It provides a platform for students to network, exchange ideas, and collaborate on projects related to facial recognition. The club also provides a forum for students to discuss the latest advancements in the field, as well as to discuss ethical and legal implications of facial recognition technologies. Face Club at Amrita University is a great opportunity for students to gain experience in the field and to make a real impact in the world.
