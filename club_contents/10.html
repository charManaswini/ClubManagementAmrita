Squad Adventure Club at the university is a student-run organization that provides outdoor recreational activities and adventures for students on campus. Our club members have the opportunity to explore the great outdoors in a safe and supportive environment. Squad Adventure Club provides a wide range of activities from hiking, kayaking, and rock climbing, to camping, fishing, and more. We also provide transportation to and from the activities, and provide knowledgeable leaders who can help with any questions about the activities and their equipment. Our mission is to provide a fun, safe, and supportive atmosphere for students to explore the outdoors. We have a variety of trips and activities to choose from, so there is something for everyone. Our club is open to all students on campus, and we look forward to seeing you at our upcoming events!
